Cardigan Welsh Corgi

The Cardigan Welsh Corgi is named after Cardigan county in Wales. They are generally larger than the Pembroke. Their ears are more rounded. They also have long tails, unlike the Pembroke.

They are between 14 to 17 kg and 27 - 32 cm tall. They come in a much wider range of colors than the Pembroke.
