Pembroke Welsh Corgi

The Pembroke Welsh Corgi is named after the county in Wales where it originated, Pembroke. It has short legs and pointed ears. They are between 11 and 14 kg and are 25 to 30 cam tall.

They are usually red, tan, black or white. Or, some combination of those colors.

They are the most popular of the corgi breeds and is the favorite dog of the Queen of England.
